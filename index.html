Crystal Collector

Every round a random number will be displayed in the Target Score section.

Clicking on the crystals adds points to your score. Each crystal is worth a different point value.

The object of the game is to increase your score until you match the Target Score. However, you lose the round if you go over the Target Score.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you but remains the same the entire round.

All crystal values and the Target Score change randomly at the beginning of every round.

Win 5 rounds to win the game. Lose 5 rounds and it's GAME OVER.
